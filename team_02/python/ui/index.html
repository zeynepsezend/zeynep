how sensi built this +
w(s) = derive( quiz, inspire ) learned from your responses
score(room, s) = w(s) × raw(room, s) weight × room data per sense
C(room) = Σ score(room, s) sum across all 6 senses
flag(s) = |w(s) − baseline(s)| > 0.25 proactive gap detection
Your quiz answers revealed your sensory priorities and living situation. Your inspire session — written description plus moodboard — mapped your aesthetic world. Sensi weighted the six comfort senses from what you shared, then compared each against evidence baselines: research-backed minimum thresholds for your profile type. Where your stated weight falls below a baseline, Sensi flags it proactively — because the data says it matters even if you haven't noticed it yet.
